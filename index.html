A smile expresses pleasure, sociability, happiness, joy, amusement, in a nutshell all the expressions which makes us alive. This concludes that it is a important attribute to any individual.

Digital Denture

In the digital denture process, your dental prosthetist uses computer-aided design and engineering tools to craft a perfectly fitting denture using impressions and a mould of your bite. ... These impressions are sent to a lab where your dentures are crafted using 3D modelling technology.

Valplast Flexible

Flexible dentures are custom-made dental installations that rely on unique products to achieve a less rigid design. These new kinds of dentures are popular for those who struggle with the conventional acrylic base of dentures that may wear, irritate the gums, induce allergic reactions or generally fail to provide a comfortable result.

Cosmetic Dentistry

A couple of our designers are dedicated to this specialization of ours. It is a latest trend in dentistry. Dental veneers are wafer-thin, custom-made shells of tooth-colored porcelain or resin that cover the front surface of the teeth.
