BIO HPP WITH COMPOSITE

A new high performance polymer, BioHPP, based on polyether-ether-ketone (PEEK) polymer was introduced as dental material for manufacturing the superstructure dentures on dental implants. It is the brand new material in the implantology sector. A division of our designers in VR2020 is dedicated to this segment as this is a potential future of digital dentistry.
