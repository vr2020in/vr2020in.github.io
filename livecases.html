Introduction of Implant Dentistry

Predictability of Fixed Prosthodontics

Implant dentistry replaces missing roots to strengthen and support synthetic teeth. Artificial roots are made from titanium metal and are surgically placed into your jawbone. A prosthetic tooth is then attached to the implant. The process restores functionality and improves overall appearance.

Implant dentistry should also be profitable for clinicians and dental laboratory technicians. Initially, as with other new technologies that require the acquisition of learned, skilled behaviors, implant restorative dentistry may not be as profitable as other aspects of restorative dentistry.
